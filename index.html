Server Rules

To ensure everyone has a great experience, please follow our community guidelines

General Rules

  • Be Respectful – No hate speech, bullying, racism, sexism, or discrimination of any kind.
  • No Cheating or Hacking – No X-ray, fly hacks, autoclickers, or any unfair advantage.
  • Keep Chat Clean – No spamming, advertising, or inappropriate content.
  • No NSFW or Inappropriate Builds – PixelHeaven is family-friendly.
  • Follow Staff Instructions – Our staff are here to help, their decisions are final.

Gameplay Rules

  • PVP Rules: Only outside claims, unless in PvP zones or arena (combat logging is bannable – report with proof).
  • Don’t Exploit Bugs – Report bugs/glitches, don’t abuse them.
  • No Lag Machines or AFK Farms – Anything that lags the server will be removed.

Additional Notes

  • Mods & Resource Packs – Only cosmetic or performance-enhancing mods allowed (OptiFine, etc.). Ask staff if unsure.
  • Consequences – Breaking rules may result in a warning, mute, kick, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on the severity.
  • Reporting – Use /report in-game or contact staff on Discord for rule violations.
  • Appeals – Banned players can appeal on our Discord server.
